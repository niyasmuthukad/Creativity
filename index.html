Storytelling - The Pen and the Pencil

A Pen laughed at a Pencil. "I am better than you," the Pen said, "because my ink lasts forever. You are weak because you can be erased." Suddenly, the Pen made a spelling mistake. He could not fix it, and the paper looked messy and ugly. Then, the Pencil made a mistake. He simply used his eraser, fixed the error, and kept writing perfectly. The Pen realized that being able to fix your mistakes is actually a strength, not a weakness.

Moral: "It is better to correct your mistakes than to be stuck with them."
